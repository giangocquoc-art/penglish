## Design rules

- Keep Apple-inspired ocean UI.
- Use glass cards lightly.
- Backgrounds must use background-size: cover and background-position: center.
- Add soft white/blue overlay when readability is low.
- Mascots should be decorative and supportive, not too large or noisy.
- Mobile and desktop must stay readable at 100% browser zoom.
- Do not show broken image icons.
- Do not use random external mascot images anymore.
- Do not deploy automatically.
